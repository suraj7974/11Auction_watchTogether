  the Database type doesn't satisfy GenericSchema. Two things were needed for
  our custom eleven_auction schema: each table needs a Relationships field, and
  the row types must be type aliases, not interfaces (interfaces aren't
  assignable to Record<string, unknown>). Without this, every .from() query
  silently typed as never.
